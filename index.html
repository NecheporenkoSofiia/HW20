Lana Del Rey

About:

American singer, songwriter and poet. Her music has been praised by critics for its cinematic style, preoccupation with tragic relationships and melancholy, and references to American popular culture, particularly the 1950s and 1960s.

Short biography:

She began her music career under the pseudonym Lizzie Grant, but gained widespread recognition with the release of the album "Born to Die" in 2012. The disc quickly became a commercial success and brought Lana world fame. The artist was noted for her characteristic voice and unique style, which combined the influences of various musical genres, including pop, rock and indie.
